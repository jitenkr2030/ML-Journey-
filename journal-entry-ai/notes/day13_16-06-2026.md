============================================================
TF-IDF VECTORIZATION
============================================================

Computers cannot understand text directly.

TF-IDF converts:

"Salary paid to employees"

into numbers.

Example:

Salary → 0.72
Paid → 0.41
Employees → 0.66

This numerical data is used by ML models.
