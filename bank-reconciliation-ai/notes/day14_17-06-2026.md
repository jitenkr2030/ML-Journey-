# Understanding Bank Reconciliation

Bank reconciliation means comparing:

### Book Records

Entries maintained inside accounting software.

Examples:

* Vendor Payment
* Salary Payment
* GST Payment
* Customer Receipt

### Bank Records

Transactions appearing in bank statements.

Goal:

Verify whether both records represent the same transaction.

# Label Encoding

Learned:

```python
LabelEncoder()
```

Purpose:

Convert status labels into numbers.

Example:

Matched → 0

Possible Match → 1

Unmatched → 2

Machine learning models require numerical targets.
