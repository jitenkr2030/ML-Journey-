## Model Evaluation Results

MAE:
17.83

RMSE:
22.41

R²:
0.8932

### Interpretation

MAE means the model prediction is off by about ₹17.83 on average.

RMSE means larger mistakes are penalized more heavily.

R² of 0.8932 means the model explains about 89.32% of the variation in stock prices.

---

## Feature Importance Results

Prev_Close   : 0.458755

Prev_Low     : 0.252879

Prev_High    : 0.188730

Prev_Open    : 0.043085

Prev_MA5     : 0.034952

Prev_Volume  : 0.021598

### Analysis

Most Important Feature:

Prev_Close

Least Important Feature:

Prev_Volume

Conclusion:

Yesterday's closing price is the strongest indicator of tomorrow's closing price.

Trading volume had very little impact on prediction performance in this project.

## Model Persistence

Learned how to save a trained model.

Example:

joblib.dump(model, "models/reliance_rf_model.pkl")

Benefits:

* No need to retrain every time
* Faster predictions
* Production-ready workflow

---

## Loading Saved Model

Example:

loaded_model = joblib.load(
"models/reliance_rf_model.pkl"
)

This allows the saved model to be reused later.

---

## Inference

Inference means using a trained model to make predictions on new data.

Workflow:
